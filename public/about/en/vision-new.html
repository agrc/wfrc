VISION FOR OUR FUTURE

Utah is growing... and we have a plan. Our future quality of life depends on the choices we make today. Wasatch Choice 2050 is our communities' chared vision for transportation investments, development patterns, and economic opportunities. The Vision map and key strategies show how advancing the Vision can enhance quality of life even as we grow.

Key Strategies

The Wasatch Choice 2050 Vision is built on four key strategies:
Provide transportation choices
Support housing options
Preserve open space
Link economic development with transportation and housing decisions
